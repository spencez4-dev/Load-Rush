LOAD RUSH V4.0 — GAMEPLAY PACK

BUILT FROM
- The pre-visual-overhaul V3.9 custom-title version.
- The visual-overhaul fork was not used.

NEW: GHOST TRUCK
- A translucent rival races at the pace of your best previous hour.
- Its position is based on where that best hour was at the same minute and second.
- It stays hidden until the app has at least one previous tracked hour.

NEW: COMBO METER
- Positive loads chained within three minutes build a combo.
- Tiers: Combo Active, Hot Streak, and Freight Frenzy.
- The meter is intentionally cosmetic and does not distort real load totals.
- Negative updates break the chain.

BETTER UNLOCKS
- Every rig now has a rarity and a named cosmetic road effect.
- Equipped rigs tint the race trail and vehicle glow.
- Newly earned rigs trigger a large unlock celebration.
- Existing users do not receive a flood of old unlock popups.
